Wetoro

For centuries, people have practiced quiet rituals to honor what they feel.

In Japan, Toro Nagashi is a ceremony where lanterns are released onto rivers at dusk. Each one carries a message — a memory, a hope, a letting go — drifting into the night as a symbolic release.

We all have moments when we need a space to express what we're feeling, without judgment or advice.

Wetoro offers a modern ritual for that same purpose, a clearing where your words dissolve and become part of something shared.
