The Clearing

For centuries, people have practised quiet rituals to release what they carry.

In Japan, a ceremony called Toro Nagashi sends lanterns onto rivers. Each lantern holds a memory, a burden, a prayer. As it drifts away, people symbolically let something go.

Sometimes what we carry is hard to share — not the right moment, or we simply need a place to release it without advice or response.

The Clearing is a modern ritual for that same purpose.
